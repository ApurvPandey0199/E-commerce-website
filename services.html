Our B2B Solutions

End-to-end industrial apparel manufacturing, custom print decoration, and logistics support.

FABRIC SELECTION

1. Custom Fabric Manufacturing

We manufacture fabric rolls based on your exact fiber specifications. Select combed cotton, heavy cotton pique, polyester sports mesh, fleece weights, or organic cotton.

Available finishes include biowash pre-shrunk treatment, softeners, moisture-wicking coatings, anti-microbial coatings, and water-repellent storm shells.

BRAND LABS

2. Branding & Customization Labs

Add your corporate branding with premium clarity. Our screen-printing labs feature automatic carousels supporting sharp plastisol, discharge, and water-based inks.

Our embroidery division features Tajima computer sewing machines, perfect for detailed high-stitch corporate badges, and metallic thread overlays.

LOGISTICS

3. Ironing, Packing & Shipping

We offer retail-ready packaging services. Each garment is steam-ironed, folded, neatly sealed inside branded polybags, and tagged with hangtags/size labels.

Our logistics networks deliver orders across the country via trusted land cargo or express air freight. Fully tracked from dispatch.
