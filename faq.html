Help & FAQs

Answers to questions regarding fabric weights, B2B invoicing, and bulk custom designs.

What are your Minimum Order Quantities (MOQ)?

Our MOQs depend on the apparel category due to setup parameters in our knitting mills and print screen calibrations:

  • Classic Crewneck T-Shirts: 50 Units
  • Pique Polo Shirts: 30 Units
  • Pullover Hoodies & Outerwear: 25 Units
  • Hospital Scrubs & Uniform Sets: 50 Units
  • Promotional Canvas Tote Bags: 100 Units
How do you calculate B2B volume discounts?

Our pricing engine scales down automatically as order volume increases. We offer structural discount tiers: 50-100 units (base tier), 101-500 units (mid tier), and 500+ units (enterprise factory tier). Pricing details are updated instantly in the live calculator on product detail pages.

Which branding customization method should I select?

We recommend methods based on the fabric type and your design details:

  • Screen Printing: Best for large bold graphic designs, event crewnecks, and high quantity promo runs. Exceptionally crisp and wash-durable.
  • Embroidery: Ideal for corporate pique polos, outerwear storm jackets, and professional chef coats. Adds a premium corporate texture.
  • DTG (Direct-to-Garment) Printing: Recommended for complex photo designs or small multi-color graphic overlays on cotton shirts.
Do you offer GST compliant invoices for input tax credit?

Yes. PR Manufacturing Co. is a fully registered entity. If you provide your corporate 15-digit GSTIN ID during checkout, it will be validated and included in your Tax Invoice, enabling your finance department to claim the 18% GST input tax credit (ITC) on the purchase.

What are your production and logistics dispatch timelines?

Once sizing parameters are confirmed, design artwork approved, and payment processed, standard orders enter the manufacturing queue. Production typically takes 7-10 business days. Transit shipping takes 3-5 days via B2B cargo logistics depending on your delivery location. Express options are available for urgent campaign deadlines.
